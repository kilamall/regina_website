About Me

Hi, I'm Regina, a Licensed Esthetician in the Bay Area. Inspired by my grandma’s red lips and my mom’s skincare rituals, I fell in love with beauty early on. I studied Economics at San Diego State, which gave me a knack for market trends. During COVID, I perfected makeup looks for the fun of it and launched Queen Bueno Beauty on Instagram, experimenting with my roommates as models. Working at Ulta Beauty as a brow specialist and makeup artist deepened my product knowledge and customer service skills. It was a hard choice then but now I blend my economic insights with my beauty expertise. Let's find the intersection between beauty and happiness. Join me to discover the art of self-care and unlock your inner beauty!
